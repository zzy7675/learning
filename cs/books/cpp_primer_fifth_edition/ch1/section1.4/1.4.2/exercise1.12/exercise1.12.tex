    keywordstyle=\bfseries\color{blue},% 关键字的样式

\section*{Exercise 1.12}

\begin{lstlisting}[language=C++]
int sum = 0;
for (int i = -100; i <= 100; ++i)
sum += i;
\end{lstlisting}

\begin{flushleft}
What the \lstinline[language=C++]|for| loop does is to sum from -100
to 100 inclusive, which ends up with \lstinline[language=C++]|sum = 0|.
\end{flushleft}
